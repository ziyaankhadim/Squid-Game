Instructions:


WELCOME...
You are a contestant in the deadly game of
Red Light Green Light
When Doll facing BACKWARD, you are free to move towards the finish line.
Any movement detected when Doll facing FORWARD will result in ELIMINATION.
The challenge is to reach the finish line without getting caught.
The game continues until either a winner reaches the finish line or player is ELIMINATED.

CONTROLS:

To move FORWARD press W or Up Arrow key
To move BACKWARD press S or Down Arrow key
To move LEFT press A or Left Arrow key
To move RIGHT press D or Right Arrow key
To JUMP press Space
